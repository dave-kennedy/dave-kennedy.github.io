Spacing

border

An element's border is composed of three properties: border-width, border-style, and border-color. 

border-width: 5px;
border-style: solid;
border-color: red;

All three properties can be set with the border shorthand property: 

border: 5px solid red;

The border on each side can be controlled separately with the following properties:

  • border-top-width
  • border-top-style
  • border-top-color
  • border-right-width
  • border-right-style
  • border-right-color
  • border-bottom-width
  • border-bottom-style
  • border-bottom-color
  • border-left-width
  • border-left-style
  • border-left-color

Or with the following shorthand properties:

  • border-top
  • border-right
  • border-bottom
  • border-left
border-top: 5px solid red;
border-right: 5px solid red;
border-bottom: 5px solid red;
border-left: 5px solid red;

margin

This property controls the amount of space around an element.

margin: 40px;

When two values are assigned, the first controls the space on the top and bottom sides, and the second controls the space on the right and left sides. 

margin: 10px 40px;

When four values are assigned, they control the space on the top, right, bottom, and left sides respectively. 

margin: 10px 20px 30px 40px;

The space on each side can also be controlled separately with the margin-top, margin-right, margin-bottom, and margin-left properties. 

margin-top: 40px;
margin-right: 40px;
margin-bottom: 40px;
margin-left: 40px;

auto

Setting a block-level element's margin-left and margin-right properties to auto centers the element horizontally within its parent. 

display: block;
margin-left: auto;
margin-right: auto;

padding

This property controls the amount of space inside an element, between its content and its border. 

padding: 40px;

When two values are assigned, the first controls the space on the top and bottom sides, and the second controls the space on the right and left sides. 

padding: 10px 40px;

When four values are assigned, they control the space on the top, right, bottom, and left sides respectively. 

padding: 10px 20px 30px 40px;

The space on each side can also be controlled separately with the padding-top, padding-right, padding-bottom, and padding-left properties. 

padding-top: 40px;
padding-right: 40px;
padding-bottom: 40px;
padding-left: 40px;

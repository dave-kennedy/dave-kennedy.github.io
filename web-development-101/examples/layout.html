display

Some elements are arranged vertically by default. These elements have a display property of block and are called block-level elements.

Other elements are arranged horizontally by default. These elements have a display property of inline and are called inline elements.

Block-level elements are as wide as their parents by default, but they can be resized by their height and width properties.

Inline elements are only as big as their contents and can't be resized.

flex

Any element with a display property of flex becomes a flex container. Its children automatically become flex items without requiring any additional styling.

Flex items can grow and shrink as needed without introducing any space between the elements.
